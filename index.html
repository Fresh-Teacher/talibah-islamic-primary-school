About Us

Twalibah Islamic Primary School is located at Kasenge - Kikajjo along Nakawuka Road.

Facts about Talibah Islamic Primary School

Below are some of the short details about Talibah Islamic Primary School.

  • We teach secular and theological studies.
  • We handle all classes from Nursery to Primary Seven.
  • We teach while following the curriculum and syllabus as directed by the Ministry of Education and Sports .

An school has a strong Islamic background and will provide your children with a firm Islamic foundation to enable them grow spiritually as Allah fearing citizens.

Services

Below are some of the services provided at Talibah Islamic Primary School.

Academics

We provide educational services to all classes ranging from Nursery to Primary Seven following the National Curriculum as per the National Curriculum Development Centre.

Religion

We teach Religious Education to our pupils and practice recitation of the Holy Quran.

Sports

Our schools participates in sports activities like soccer and we have won numerous trophies during different competitions.

Prayers

Our school is very strict on religion and we make sure that our pupils pray the five obligatory prayers.

  • Games and Sports

    Our school participates in games and sports competitions and it has won many trophies during those competitions.

  • Players

    We have a well trained team of football players who have made our school famours by winning different football competitions.

  • Team Work

    We have a very dedicated team of players who work together for the best performance during the games.

  • Experience

    Our football team has well experienced players who achieve it through practice.

Frequently asked questions about Talibah Day and Boarding Primary School

Below are some of the FAQs about our school together with their answers

  • 01 Does Talibah Islamic Primary School have a nursery section?

    Yes! Our school has a section for infants i.e Baby Class, Middle Class and Top Class.

  • 02 Does Talibah Islamic Primary School have a boarding section?

    Yes! Our school has both day and boarding pupils and provides accomodation to boarding pupils.

  • 03 Does Talibah Islamic Primary School only admit Muslim Pupils?

    No! In spite of the fact that we are a Muslim school, we also welcome all pupils with different doctrines.
